Let’s Hack Shack!
After years of only being able to meet virtually, the HPE Developer Community team is excited to be able to bring the Hack Shack back next month – in person – at the HPE Discover 2022 Edge-to-Cloud conference in Las Vegas, June 28-20.
The team is gearing up, designing a whole bunch of new role-based challenges, branded swag, in-person meetup sessions, and more. In this month’s edition, get specifics on our newest challenges and a preview of what you’ll see in the Hack Shack!
