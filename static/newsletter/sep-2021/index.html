People make a community
Sometimes we get so lost in technology that we forget that there are real people behind these advances. HPE Developer is all about building community, something that is, admittedly, made more difficult with today’s social distancing. And yet, continuing to closely engage and collaborate with people is more important than ever before.

This month we introduce several members of the community via our different programs. Meet one developer as he shares his exploration through the HPE Developer portal. Meet another in a blog interview. Watch more in video replays as they participate in Munch & Learn sessions or guide you through the HPE DEV site. And don’t forget that you can converse with us on Slack or in the HPE Ezmeral Software Forum. Remember, we’re here for each other.
