HPE GreenLake
We had a blast connecting with community members at the HPE Discover 2022 Edge-to-Cloud conference – both old and new! And it was exciting to be able to share the newly branded HPE GreenLake edge-to-cloud platform with all attendees.
At the event, we revealed our newly revamped HPE Developer Community portal and introduced the beta version of the new HPE GreenLake Developer portal. Here, HPE GreenLake customers and partners can take advantage of our well-documented, secure, and scalable framework of APIs for the HPE GreenLake edge-to-cloud platform. Contents in this resource portal will grow rapidly, so make sure you check back often.
