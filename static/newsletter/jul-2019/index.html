Beyond HPE Discover Las Vegas '19
Hey, it’s Alex Mejias again, returning from another successful HPE Discover Las Vegas. Over the course of the event, I spoke with 1,000+ developers, designers, customers, and partners, listening to their pain points and finding ways to collaborate to solve issues. The HPE DEV & Design Hack Shack was one of the top 3 most popular areas at HPE Discover and the energy there was unmistakable. Hack Shack Attack! our 8-bit arcade game, boasted over 1,400 game plays. Imagine all that IT monster taming! HPE’s customers welcomed the opportunity to speak with us about how important designing for the user experience (UX) has become and to attend our numerous technical sessions. Check out our post-event blogs and our Hack Shack landing page for a full recap of all the workshops, challenges, and fun.

With all the focus around HPE Discover, you may be wondering if that’s all HPE DEV has been up to. Hardly! We’ve been participating in open source developer and design events like OSCON and KubeCon, and several other very exciting things planned for the second half of FY19. We’re working on some great new open source projects and engaging technical content. Follow our progress and stay up-to-date by visiting the HPE DEV portal, connecting with us on Slack, and reading these monthly newsletters.
