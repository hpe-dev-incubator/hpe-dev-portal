Secure and simplify
Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) invests heavily in technologies designed to secure and simplify your workloads. In this month’s edition, we offer a tutorial on how to use the new persona views to deploy services in HPE GreenLake for private cloud. We also explore new deep learning tools built specifically for data scientists.
In other news, we’ve just launched a new workshop that, through a fun hands-on experience, walks you through the fundamentals of Docker. And we’re offering two new technology talks this month focused on securing and modernizing your apps; one on Mithril: Istio + SPIRE and another on vFunction. Read on to learn more!
