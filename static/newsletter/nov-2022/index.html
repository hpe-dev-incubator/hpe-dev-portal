Contributing as a gift
Technology can aid in improving the way people live and work. Many times the contributions we make to technology give us something back in the process.
In this month’s edition, we highlight the gifts of software contributors – many supporting open source projects – and learn how the experience impacted them. We also explore low-code/no-code platforms, ways for even those without deep code experience to take advantage of software enablement.
A replay of our October Munch & Learn on HPE’s sustainability research and strategy explains how HPE works to give a better world to succeeding generations. Maybe this month’s edition will inspire you to make your own contributions.
