Monthly #81
Building and handling modern applications can be fraught with complexity. This month, we feature solutions to scale and orchestrate your applications through a union of HPE GreenLake for Private Cloud Enterprise and Kubernetes. We also show you ways to make them highly available and how to take advantage of single sign-on features.

In addition, we spotlight Chapel, an open source programming language that simplifies parallel programming for productive parallel computing at scale. Learn more about its development, and take advantage of an opportunity to make it even better!

And make sure you check out our new Workshop-on-Demand that offers a hands-on experience with Podman, an open source tool developed by Red Hat that makes it easy to find, run, build, share and deploy applications using containers on Linux systems.
