Monthly #74
This month’s newsletter is focused on learning – everything from learning how to use Terraform for bare metal provisioning on HPE GreenLake to building custom Docker images for EMR serverless. Our blog posts are a great way to gain this level of expertise.

Workshops are another great way to learn new technology skills. In this month’s newsletter, we continue our series on how you can develop your own Workshop-on-Demand program, similar to the one we’ve been using for our hands-on workshops.

We also highlight another method HPE uses to encourage a love for learning with younger students through hackathons. And don’t forget the value of events and webinars… this month you’ll hear about the HPE Machine Learning Development Environment (MLDE) in our monthly meetup. Happy learning!!
