Destination: Discover
Hi all. Alex here again. HPE DEV has been hard at work preparing for Hewlett Packard Enterprise’s (HPE) premier event – HPE Discover, June 18-20 in Las Vegas. The event, which brings together thousands of people from all over the world, is a chance for HPE to showcase its latest and greatest technology and solutions. Our whole team has been involved in expanding the Hack Shack experience with improved technology and design workshops complimented with exciting code challenges.

Last year’s HPE Hack Shack at HPE Discover in Madrid was a huge hit. At any point during the event, you could find people sitting with their laptops completely engaged in the hands-on workshops and code challenges. Our new booth design replaces the Hack Shack backyard with a welcoming front yard where you can relax in lawn chairs and participate in fun activities. Attendees can play our Hack Shack Attack, a retro 8-bit arcade game. Passersby can check out the leaderboard to see who’s currently poised to win the grand prize, which will be given away at our awards ceremony on Wednesday, June 19th, from 5-6 pm. We hope you stop by to see how the HPE DEV and Design teams are enabling developers and designers through awesome open source projects, docs, and resources.

Of course, that’s not all we’ve been doing this month. Take a moment to browse through the latest newsletter to learn about our other HPE DEV activities.
