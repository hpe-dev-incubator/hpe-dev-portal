Sharing expertise
Sharing knowledge to speed innovation is key to advancing the way people live and work. This month, HPE DEV Community members offer several tutorials designed to help you harness data from edge to cloud and extract more insight out of your data. There’s also a Prometheus exporter early access program that you’re invited to participate in where you can provide feedback to help enhance the product.

Of course, nothing beats being able to connect in person and share what’s new. You’ll finally get that opportunity at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon in Los Angeles, CA. Read Sir Hackington’s post to learn more!

Sign up for the Prometheus exporter early access program to test out correlating the performance and capacity of your storage with application performance and utilization through a single pane of glass.
